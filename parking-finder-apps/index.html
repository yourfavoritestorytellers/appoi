Which are best parking finder apps for iOS and Android devices

by Team AppsPoint · Published · Updated November 21, 2019

Parking finder apps for iOS and Android smartphones are really helpful to save your time and money. There are two problems for people living in overcrowded areas related with parking, first to find the place for parking and second is to relocate your car within a huge parking facility. One can find both free and paid parking space with the help of parking finder apps listed here.

Some of apps even allow to save a parking spot to save your time and money also by comparing some available options & selecting right one. Some apps also allows you to find back your car location by using your phone GPS while giving you peace of mind. Below are some options available as best parking finder applications for Android and iOS smartphones.

Parking finder apps for iOS and Android

1. ParkMe

Best parking finder app available in the market with more than 84,000 parking locations worldwide. Compare available spots and save money when you go with a cheap option. When you link a credit card it let you pay in advance and reserve a location to save your time in finding a parking point. You can also select any monthly parking options available nearby.

Download for iOS or Android

2. SpotHero

Another app to find parking nearby, users can search for hourly, monthly, and airport locations. You can find near by places, then compare prices before selecting any option. One can also book for future by simply prepaying for your location and get the pass delivered by SMS. Email or on App. You can also get discounts and offers for various parking locations.

Download for iOS or Android

3. ParkingPanda

Another reliable source to find a parking location nearby, the app mainly focus on providing service by partnering with lots and garages through out U.S. and Canada. The service is recommended by Forbes, and its idea makes it different from other options. It also offers price comparison, and pre-booking feature just like others do.

Download for iOS or Android

4. BestParking

Top recommended app from many users of this service, it is covering 105 cities across North America. With the app you can find all the available options nearby to compare and select the best offer. It also let you to find a spot at airports, and pre-booking also when you pay in advance. With around 1 million monthly users the app manages to grow further and new parking places are often on the way.

Download for iOS or Android

Other Parking Apps to Consider

If you are not satisfied with the above parking apps, and looking for some other options. Then you may need to try the following apps, some of these apps are city specific also, and offer services within specific cities. This list contains following names;

  1. Parking Mate
  2. ParkWhiz
  3. VoicePark
  4. ParkChicago
